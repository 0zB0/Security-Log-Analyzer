# Code of Conduct

## Expected Conduct

Be respectful, technically specific, and constructive. Review ideas and code on their evidence,
security impact, maintainability, and reproducibility. Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory
language, doxxing, and publication of private logs, credentials, exploit details, or personal data
are not accepted.

## Security And Privacy

Do not place vulnerability details or sensitive evidence in public issues, discussions, pull
requests, screenshots, or test fixtures. Use the private process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, close, or reject contributions that violate these boundaries and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns privately through the
contact method published at <https://ozbejbohanec.com>.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.
